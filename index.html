How UGC Pricing Works in 2026

Views Factor

More average views mean higher conversion potential for brands. A creator with 1M views commands a significant premium over one with 10K views, acting as a multiplier on your base rate.

Niche Factor

High-ROI niches like Finance and Tech pay premium rates because their customer lifetime value is higher. Beauty and Fashion are highly saturated, meaning rates are often closer to the baseline.

Usage Rights Factor

If a brand uses your video in paid ads (whitelisting/dark posting) or demands exclusivity, they must pay for the license. Never give away perpetuity rights without a 50-100% markup.

UGC Pricing Tips for 2026

  • Never do raw footage for free: Brands can edit 10 videos from your raw clips. Charge +30% for raw footage.
  • Bundle and save: Offer 3-video and 5-video packages to increase your Average Order Value (AOV).
  • Negotiate usage terms: Standard is 90-days paid ad usage. Anything longer should be renewed at 20-30% of the original rate.
  • Hook variations: Offer 1 video with 3 different hook (first 3 seconds) variations as an easy upsell.
  • Stay updated: The 2026 algorithm favors authentic, low-production value content over studio setups.
